# Trends
- **Hyperproductivity** is a new mode of work where individuals use AI as a system that they continuously refine to automate entire workflows.
- Early adopters build custom AI-driven pipelines that **compound in efficiency**, enabling small teams to produce far more than traditional organizations.
- This style of work is still niche, demanding high skill, constant oversight, and currently working best on **small or greenfield projects** rather than mature systems.
